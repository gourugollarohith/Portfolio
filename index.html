About

A bit about how I work

I'm a Senior Power BI Developer with nearly five years of experience designing enterprise Business Intelligence solutions — the kind that stakeholders open every week, not the kind that gets built once and forgotten. At Cognizant, I've built interactive dashboards, optimized data models, and automated reporting processes using Power BI, DAX, Power Query, Microsoft Fabric, SQL, and Power Automate.

"A dashboard that looks good but nobody trusts is worse than no dashboard at all — my job is to make the numbers defensible, not just visible."

That means getting the unglamorous parts right: clean semantic models, Row-Level Security that actually holds up, and DAX that doesn't fall over the moment the dataset grows. I'm Microsoft Certified in PL-300 and DP-600 (Fabric Analytics Engineer Associate), and I care more about a dashboard being correct and used than about it winning a design award.

Gen AI Dashboard

Tracking a company-wide AI upskilling program

Cognizant needed a single, trustworthy view of how 3.5+ Lakhs of associates were progressing through an AI learning path — from beginner to AI Builder. I built the dashboard that leadership now checks weekly: interactive KPIs, learning-path matrices, and a semantic model optimized a Matrix of 14 KPIs which loads from 30+ secs to 10-12 secs even as the dataset grew. It replaced a patchwork of spreadsheets with one governed, reusable source of truth.

ALADIN Dashboard

Replacing five reports with one, without losing trust

Learning progress, certification status, and license utilization were spread across five separate Power BI reports — each maintained differently, each a little less trusted than the last. I consolidated them into one interactive dashboard with Row-Level Security so every associate up to Director level sees exactly what they should. Automated refreshes and Microsoft subscriptions now save roughly 1,350 hours of manual reporting work every year as they can use "Global Filters Interact with '5 dashboards' with 'single selection'"
